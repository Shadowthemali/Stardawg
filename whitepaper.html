Overview

What StarDawg Is

A concise public document for the project’s identity, supply structure, and next steps.

StarDawg is a Solana utility token built around a recognizable brand, a community-first message, and a roadmap that can evolve without drifting off mission.

The goal is to launch with clarity, keep the structure easy to verify, and add utility in measured phases as the community matures.

Purpose

Utility and Value

Why the token exists beyond the launch moment.

Community utility

DAWG can support access, participation, community recognition, and future features that reward engagement without making the token dependent on hype.

Brand utility

Plan

Roadmap

Five clear phases from foundation to expansion.

Phase 0

Foundation

Lock the brand, publish the official site, finalize public messaging, and make the project easy to verify and follow.

Phase 1

Launch

Build awareness, drive initial traffic, and make the website the central source for official information and links.

Phase 2

Community Growth

Turn launch attention into an active recurring community with content, engagement loops, and contributor pathways.

Phase 3

Utility Experiments

Test small useful features such as gated content, lightweight rewards, or community tools without overcomplicating the project.

Phase 4

Ecosystem Expansion

Expand partnerships, refine the site and docs, and evolve the project into a recognizable ecosystem brand.
